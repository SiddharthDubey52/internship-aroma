    Job Position

    We are seeking a creative and talented graphic designer to work collaboratively with our marketing team to create visually appealing content that will promote our brand and products.     - Strong design portfolio showcasing both print and digital designs
    - Strong communication and collaboration skills, particularly working in a team environment
    - Ability to manage multiple design projects simultaneously while meeting deadlines
    - Excellent attention to detail and ability to deliver high-quality design work.
